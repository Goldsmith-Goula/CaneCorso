Adoption Terms and Conditions
Health Guarantee

We provide a 1-year health guarantee against genetic disorders. The puppy must be examined by a licensed veterinarian within 72 hours of delivery for the guarantee to be valid.

Return Policy

If for any reason you cannot keep the puppy, you must return it to us. We do not offer refunds but will help rehome the puppy.

Spay/Neuter Agreement

If the puppy is not already spayed/neutered, you agree to have this done by 6 months of age and provide proof to us.

Deposit Policy

A non-refundable deposit of $300 is required to reserve a puppy, which will be applied to the total adoption fee.

Shipping Policy

Standard shipping ensures your puppy arrives safely within 3-5 business days. Hand-delivery provides a dedicated nanny to accompany your puppy on their flight for maximum comfort and safety.
